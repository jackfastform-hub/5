Vector Graphics Projects

Vector Graphics 1

This assignment was my first time working with vector graphics using Adobe Illustrator. In this project, I practiced basic design skills such as changing the size of shapes, adjusting stroke weight, and using different colours to create a complete design. This was my favourite assignment in the unit because it was the first project I completed, and I spent a lot of time experimenting with different shapes and layouts. Even though it may not be the most visually advanced piece, it was an important learning experience and helped me build a strong foundation for future vector graphics projects.

Vector Graphics 3

This assignment focused on practicing the pen tool in Adobe Illustrator. Overall, the two designs were not very difficult. The heart shape was easier to create because it mainly used smooth and simple curves. The dog design was more challenging, especially at the turning points and when adjusting the layer order near the end of the project. This assignment helped me improve my control of curves and better understand how layering works in Illustrator.

Vector Graphics 5

This assignment introduced the scale tool, shear tool, rotate tool, and reflect tool, and allowed me to practice using each of them in Adobe Illustrator. The difficulty level of this project was not very high, but it helped me better understand how these transformation tools work. Even though the task was simple, it was very helpful and provided a strong foundation for future assignments that required more complex transformations.

Vector Graphics - Text on Path

In this assignment, the goal was to learn how to use the Text on Path tool. For my design, I arranged text along curved paths to form the shape of a bunny, using the text to outline features such as the ears, face, and body. I mainly used the pen tool to create smooth paths so the text could follow the shapes accurately. This project helped me understand how text can be used as both a design element and a way to define form. It also improved my confidence with vector tools and prepared me for the vector graphics summative.

Vector Graphics Summative Assignment

In this assignment, I created a vector portrait of the celebrity Ye Zhou using Adobe Illustrator. I began by setting up the canvas, importing a reference image, and adding proportion guidelines to help with the facial structure. I used the pen tool to block out the main shapes, including the face, hair, clothing, and background, and applied flat colours without outlines. I then added shadows and highlights using the Pathfinder tool to build form. One challenge was placing shadows naturally, which I overcame by reviewing classroom tutorials. Overall, this project helped me improve my Illustrator skills and better understand shading and portrait design.

Audio Editing Projects

Audio Commercial / PSA Assignment

In this assignment, we were required to create two commercials or PSAs, including writing scripts and recording our own voice-overs. This was our first time working in a professional recording studio, and the process felt quite stressful at the beginning. The most challenging part was keeping the final audio at exactly 30 seconds and successfully blending the sound effects with my voice so that everything sounded natural and smooth. However, as I became more familiar with the process, the final result exceeded my expectations. Overall, this assignment was a valuable learning experience that helped me gain more confidence in recording and audio editing.

Audio Editing for Film Sumative (Ratatouille)

In this assignment, we were required to add appropriate sound effects to a silent film. I chose to use sound effects from Ratatouille. The most challenging part was incorporating all the details I observed, especially selecting sound effects that matched the actions on screen. For example, when seasoning was added in the film, I needed to carefully choose sounds that were similar and realistic. I also focused on using subtle background sounds to enhance the atmosphere of the scene. Although the process was complex and required careful attention, I was very satisfied with the final result.

Dangers of Free Apps (TikTok)

For this assignment, I chose the topic of the hidden dangers of TikTok. Through this project, I gained a deeper understanding of what TikTok is and the major risks associated with it. These risks include privacy violations and data mining, algorithm manipulation, mental health and addiction, as well as misinformation and fake content. In addition, I learned some useful safety tips and analyzed the impact of TikTok on people today by reviewing news articles and data. This assignment helped me better understand both the influence and the potential dangers of social media.

After Effects Projects

Adobe After Effects 1

This was my first introduction to Adobe After Effects, where I learned the basic interface and core animation principles. I practiced creating simple motion graphics, keyframe animation, and layer management. The project focused on understanding timing and spacing in animations, as well as how to create smooth transitions between elements. This assignment laid the foundation for more complex motion design projects and helped me develop a better eye for dynamic visual storytelling.
